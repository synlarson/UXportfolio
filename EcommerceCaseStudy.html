Hypothesis:

We believe that people who grocery shop online have a need for saving time retrieving groceries. We know we have succeeded when the navigation of Central Market's checkout process has become more time-efficient and effective. This will contribute to a higher task completion rate and a decrease in time spent while checking out.

User Research

Heuristics Evaluation

We started off by doing a heuristic evaluation on the current Central Market Homepage.

Here's what we noticed:

The Hompage is a blog format instead of e-commerce.
Too many cards that add extra clicks to the user experience
Navigation bar repeats the cards on the homepage which seems redundant
The log in and Sign up button could be consolidated into one.
